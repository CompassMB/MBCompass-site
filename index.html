MBCompass is a modern, free, and open source Compass and Navigation app without Ads, IAP, or Tracking. That's support Compass and Navigation features with being lightweight and simple!

Clear Direction

MBCompass clearly shows the cardinal direction, along with magnetic strength, to help you navigate with ease.

Live Location Tracking

Show your live GPS location on OpenStreetMap for hiking or travel, no tracking, no data stored.

Accuracy

MBCompass utilizes multiple sensors (Sensor Fusion) to provide accurate readings, ensuring you never lose your way.

Modern & Efficient

MBCompass uses the latest Material Design and Jetpack Compose, following Android lifecycle for a smooth, efficient experience.

Convenience Features

MBCompass supports landscape orientation and keeps the screen on, offering uninterrupted usability during travel or hiking.

Open Source

MBCompass is 100% open source, so you can view the code and contribute to.
